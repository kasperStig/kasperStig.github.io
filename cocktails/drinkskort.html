Negroni
Gin | Sweet Vermouth | Campari
Negroni
  • 4cl Gin
  • 4cl Sweet Vermouth
  • 4cl Campari

Add together, stir with ice, strain into rocks glass with ice.

Variation: Swap 4cl Campari with 2cl Campari and 2cl Quantro i tres

Clover Club
Gin | Lemon | Raspberry
Clover Club
  • 6cl Gin
  • 2cl Lemon Juice
  • 2cl Simple Syrup
  • 1 Egg White
  • 7-8 Raspberries

Mix gin, lemon juice, syrup, 5ish raspberries and shake with ice

Double strain into a glass, empty the shaker and add pour the drink back into the shaker with the egg white

Shake and pour straight into a coup glass

Garnish with raspberries

Tommy's Margarita
Tequila | Lemon | Agave Syrup
Tommy's Margarita
  • 5cl Tequila
  • 3,5cl Lemon Juice
  • 1,5cl Agave Syrup

Mix and shake with ice

Double strain into a rocks glass with ice

Rum Old Fashioned
Rum | Syrup | Bitters
Rum Old Fashioned
  • 6cl Rum
  • 1cl Simple Syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Slice of lemon or orange

Stir syrup, bitters and 2cl of rum in a rocks glass with a bit of ice

Add 2cl rum and a bit of ice and stir again

Add 2cl rum and a bit of ice and stir again

Fill with ice and express the oils of the lemon / orange and rim the glass

The Bee's Knees
Gin | Lemon | Honey
The Bee's Knees
  • 6cl Gin
  • 2cl Honey Syrup
  • 2cl Lemon Juice
  • Slice of lemon

Mix and shake with ice

Double strain into a coup glass

Express the oils of the lemon and rim the glass

Variation: Try with 4cl gin

Whiskey Sour
Whiskey | Lemon | Syrup
Whiskey Sour
  • 5cl Whiskey
  • 2cl Syrup
  • 3cl Lemon Juice
  • Egg White
  • 2 dash Angostura Bitters

Mix and dry shake whiskey, syrup, lemon juice and egg white

Add ice and shake some more

Double strain into a coup glass

Garnish with two dashes of angostura bitters

Bee Sting
Tequila | Honey | Jalapeño
Bee Sting
  • 5cl Tequila
  • 2cl Honey Syrup
  • 2cl Lemon Juice
  • 1-2 Slice of Jalapeño

Muddle jalapeño gently in the shaker tin

Add the rest and shake with ice

Double strain into a martini glass

Variation: Try with a botanical gin!
